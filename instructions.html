Project 3 Instructions: Minesweeper

  • For my project 3, I am recreating the iconic Minesweeper game.
  • I recreated all of the original sprites myself so they look authentic.
  • To play, select one of the difficulty modes. They change mine count and board size.
  • If you don't know the rules of Minesweeper, click here: Rules
  • Note: Not everything in the linked website is implemented yet
  • Press R to restart, and space while in game to switch between remove mode, flag mode, and question mode.
  • When in flag mode, clicking on a tile will place a flag, but won't remove it.
  • Using the flag mode to mark down where you think the mines are.
  • Question mode is the same as flag mode, but is meant for you to mark down spots where you're unsure.
  • Flags, on the other hand, are more useful to label your final guesses.
  • You win when you reveal every non-mine tile.
