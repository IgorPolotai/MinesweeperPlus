Project 3 Proposal: Minesweeper

  • High Concept: In my Minesweeper Project, I will attempt to recreate the iconic Windows game. I plan to have a fully playable game, and a lot of custom options to have different games. I may try to recreate the original artstyle, or I may create a unique artstyle.
  • Genre: Puzzle Game.
  • Platform: Desktop Web, since on mobile it may be really hard to press the small squares
  • Story: No Story
  • Aesthetics: Simple pixel artstyle, similar to the original game
  • Gameplay: Players will have two main actions: marking squares with flags, and clicking squares to get rid of them. Clicking a mine ends the game, and you win if you click all of the non-mine squares.
  • About the developer: My name is Igor Polotai. I am a GDD and History double major at RIT, with an interest in anything art, story, music, or design related. During my free time, I like studying history.
